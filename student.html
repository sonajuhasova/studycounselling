What is study counselling
The study counselling at Business Academy Aarhus can help you with information about our programmes and we will be your sparring partner throughout your studies.

Study counselling can help with:

  • Information about the different programmes at Business Academy Aarhus
  • Application and admission
  • Study clarification and planning
  • Career counselling
  • Personal coaching
